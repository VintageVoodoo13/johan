Translation

Translation one document from one language to another is not only about perfect grammar and vocabulary. A high-quality translation also requires knowledge of the relevant field, for example, medicine, legal technical or marketing. All translators at Zealand Communication have the target languages as their mother tongue and are expert translators in their field. All translations are proofread by a second translator to ensure the highest possible quality.

Interpretation

Business meeting with guests from a foreign country? A cultural event with international guests? A court case with experts from abroad? Or maybe just a visit to a doctor during your holiday? There are many situations where language barriers make vocal communication difficult. Whether you need simultaneous or consecutive interpretation, you need an interpreter on-site or through video calls, Zealand Communication offers highly skilled and experienced interpreters.

Transcreation

Sometimes you want to communicate a feeling or a situation. You describe it in a way that brings that feeling to you but might not relatable to your target group. In the Nordic countries, a nice cozy evening could be described with words like snow and fireplace, but in Asia, those words would not bring out those feelings. Other words are much more relevant there. Transcreation is translating and adapting a text to cultural and local surroundings.

Voice-Over

Sometimes subtitling is not enough. Some people, for example, children, might not be able to read the subtitles fast enough. Sometimes you need to add some authenticity to a video clip or maybe make a commercial more effective by having the dialogue replaced to the local language. Zealand Communication has experts in voice-over who are skilled in both using their voices to express emotions as well as perfection when it comes to timing.

Transcription

Taking notes at meetings, classes at school or at interviews might be normal for most, but sometimes you need to know exactly what has been said and an audio recording is needed. Transcription is the process of transforming the audio into text. Often a time demanding and boring task, so let Zealand Communication handle it for you.
